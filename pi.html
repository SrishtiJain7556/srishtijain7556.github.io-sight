Principal Investigator

Dr Pankaj K Jain (PhD)

Dr Pankaj K Jain has over 15 years of experience in AI, machine learning, biomedical engineering, and medical imaging. He earned his PhD from IIT (BHU), Varanasi.

He started his career as a Lecturer at NIT Raipur. Later he joined Washington University in St. Louis as a Postdoctoral Research Associate in precision oncology and radiotheranostics.

He has also worked at Jio Institute, Navi Mumbai and IIT Mandi as a Project Scientist on deep learning for carotid artery imaging.

He is an Editorial Board Member of BMC Medical Informatics and Decision Making and Associate Editor of The Open Neuroimaging Journal.
