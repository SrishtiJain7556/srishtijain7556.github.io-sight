Principal Investigator

Dr Pankaj K Jain

PhD | Principal Investigator, SIGHT Lab
Artificial Intelligence Medical Imaging Machine Learning Biomedical Engineering

Dr Pankaj K Jain has over 15 years of experience in academia and research across artificial intelligence, machine learning, biomedical engineering, and medical imaging. He earned his PhD from the Indian Institute of Technology (BHU), Varanasi.

He began his academic career as a Lecturer at the National Institute of Technology, Raipur. He later joined Washington University in St. Louis, USA, as a Postdoctoral Research Associate, where he contributed to interdisciplinary research in precision oncology and radiotheranostics.

He has also worked with Jio Institute, Navi Mumbai, as a Research Associate in computer vision for medical imaging, and with IIT Mandi as a Project Scientist focusing on deep learning models for carotid artery imaging.

Recognition & Editorial Roles

Dr Jain has been recognized as a keynote speaker at IWSAB 2023, served on the Executive Council of the Washington University Postdoctoral Society, and received awards at conferences hosted by IIT Kanpur and IIT Madras. His research has been published in leading venues including Springer, Elsevier, and MDPI.

He serves as an Editorial Board Member of BMC Medical Informatics and Decision Making (Springer Nature) and Associate Editor of The Open Neuroimaging Journal (Bentham Science).
